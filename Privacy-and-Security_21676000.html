Qeo Open Source Project Documentation : Privacy and Security

Created and last modified by lissensj on May 26, 2014

Goal

The goal of Qeo's privacy and security framework is to provide

  • Authentication: reliable verification of the identity of the participants in the information exchange.
  • Access control: enforcement of different privilege levels associated with individual users. 

More Information

For more information, refer to the Introduction to Qeo Security.
